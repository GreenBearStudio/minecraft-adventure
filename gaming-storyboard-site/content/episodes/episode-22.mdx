## Not just any day..

Hunjvo woke up buzzing with excitement. Today wasn’t just any day — today his legendary friend Noor had come to visit the Fortress of Fluffy Socks. Efe was in the background, looking through some stuff..

<AssetImage name="Noor&Efe.png" />

Noor the greatest.

Noor the pro.

Noor the—

“Enough,” Noor said, waving a hand. But even then, he couldn’t help smirking a little.

He looked at Hunjvo with a spark in his eyes.

“Today,” Noor declared, “we go to the place where you were scared. Today we find potions. Today we face the mysterious goo you talked about. And just so you know… it’s called the Deep Dark. A place of ancients. A place of mystery.”

Hunjvo’s eyes widened.

The Deep Dark…

The place he had once peeked into and immediately run away from.

Noor continued, pacing dramatically like a storyteller on stage.

“The ancient builders were brave. They were bold. They were explorers! Unlike the current generation — not adventurous enough, not brave enough, not pro enough—”

Hunjvo watched in awe, nodding rapidly.

He munched on bread.

Then, of course, on rotten zombie flesh.

He offered some to Noor.

Noor took it.

Noor ate it.

Hunjvo nearly fainted from admiration.

## The Descent Begins

They met up with the other player — Efe, calm and quiet, but clearly ready for adventure. Together, the trio marched toward the cave entrance that led down… down… down into the darkness.

Hunjvo swallowed hard.

He remembered the strange goo.

The pulsing blue lights.

The whispers.

The fear.

<AssetImage name="d282_DeepDark.png" />

But with Noor and Efe beside him, he felt braver.

## Into the Deep Dark

The cave grew colder.

Quieter.

Darker.

Their torches flickered.

The air felt heavy.

The stone beneath their feet vibrated with something ancient.

Then—

A sound.

A low, distant rumble.

A heartbeat that wasn’t theirs.

Hunjvo froze.

“What was that noise?!”

Noor’s face changed instantly.

He knew.

He knew what was coming.

“RUN!” Noor shouted.

Efe sprinted.

Noor sprinted.

Hunjvo… did not sprint.

He spun in circles, confused, unsure which direction to go.

The darkness thickened.

The air trembled.

Something massive stirred in the shadows.

Something ancient.

Something blind.

Something that listened.

The Deep Dark awakened.
