## A side story of confusion, chaos, and… missing bread.

Hunjvo blinked.

One moment he was dancing with Noor and Efe, celebrating their escape from the Deep Dark.

The next moment — POOF — he was standing in the snowy courtyard of the Fortress of Fluffy Socks.

Noor and Efe were gone.

Happy the polar bear was gone.

Even the bread chest looked suspiciously empty.

Hunjvo scratched his head.
“Uhmm… what?”

He didn’t know how he got back.

He didn’t know where his friends went.

He didn’t know why everything smelled faintly of sweet berries and polar bear fur.

But he did know one thing:

He had to find Noor and Efe.

## Back Into the Deep Dark

So Hunjvo marched right back to the cave entrance.

Down the stairs.

Past the torches.

Into the cold, ancient silence.

The Deep Dark greeted him like an old nightmare.

Slimy sculk spread across the floor.

Torches from their previous escape flickered weakly.

Strange whispers echoed through the stone halls.

“Noor? Efe?” Hunjvo whispered.

<AssetVideo name="d298_DeepDarkAmbush.mp4" />

Only bats answered, flapping overhead like confused, squeaky shadows.

Then he saw it.

A Shrieker.

<AssetImage name="d317_DeepDarkSkulkShrieker.png" />

The device that summons Wardens.

Hunjvo froze.

He tip‑toed.

He held his breath.

And then—

SHRIEK.

The ground trembled.

The air thickened.

The darkness pulsed.

“Oh no…” the narrator whispered.

## The Warden Awakens

A rumble.

A crack in the earth.

And then — rising from the ground like a nightmare blooming — the Warden emerged.

Black.

Blue.

Massive.

Breathing like a furnace.

## Meanwhile… a Thousand Blocks Away

Noor and Efe sat in a boat, drifting across a calm ocean.

Happy the polar bear sat in the back, licking fish off his paws.

They munched on apples, sweet berries, and — the last of Hunjvo’s bread.

Noor tossed the final crumbs into the water without thinking.

Both he and Efe froze.

“…Did you just—”

“…I didn’t mean—”

They looked at each other.

“Well,” Noor said, “we know where to go to get more bread.”

They both burst into laughter.

Another Hunjvo‑Noor‑Efe adventure was waiting for them —
and Hunjvo had absolutely no idea.
