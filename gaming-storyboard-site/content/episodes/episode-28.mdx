## Today was the day.

Hunjvo, after losing Noor and Efe somewhere out in the world (a thousand blocks away, but Hunjvo didn’t know north from south, let alone which thousand), decided he needed… company. Companions. Helpers. A village of his own.

Or, as Hunjvo called it:
“Inviting new friends!”  

As the narrator called it:
“Kidnapping.”

## The Operation: Acquire Villagers

Hunjvo traveled far — far enough that no one in the new village had ever heard of “Hunjvo the Smelly,” “Hunjvo the Bread Thief,” or “Hunjvo the Guy Who Once Tried to Ride a Cow.”

Perfect.

He approached quietly.

He pulled out… a boat.

The cleric squinted at him.

Suspicious.

Judging.

Already backing away.

Hunjvo panicked.

He had to act fast.

THUNK.  

Boat placed.

Cleric trapped.

“HAHAHA!” Hunjvo shrieked. “You are villager number ONE!”

The cleric was horrified.

Then —

BONK.  

Another villager walked right into the boat and got stuck too.

“Hi,” said the second villager.

“Is this a party boat?”

The cleric stared at him like, Sir, we are prisoners.  

But Minecraft boats do not care about feelings.

Hunjvo was thrilled.

Two villagers!

The beginning of the Grand Village of the Fortress of Fluffy Socks.

## The Journey Home

Hunjvo talked the entire way back.

<AssetVideo name="d326_KidnappedVillagers.mp4" />

“You’ll love it! It’s high up! Very high! Snow everywhere! Polar bears! The world looks tiny from up there! It’s cold! Very cold! Sometimes you slip and fall but it’s fine!”

The villagers’ eyes grew wider with every sentence.

“No sun?”

“High? I’m afraid of heights!”

“What is snow? Why is it white? Why is it cold? Why are we going there?”

“Where is the grass? I want grass!”

But Hunjvo was too excited to notice.

Night fell.

Hunjvo had to leave them briefly to fight mobs.

The villagers sat in the dark, trapped in a boat, listening to zombies groan nearby.

Their eyes the next morning…

Let’s not talk about it.

## Arrival at the Fortress

At last, they reached the Fortress of Fluffy Socks.

Hunjvo proudly dragged the boat up the mountain (don’t ask how), placed beds, and declared:

“WELCOME TO YOUR NEW HOME!”

<AssetImage name="d326_FortressFirstVillagers.png" />

The cleric immediately ran to a composter and became a farmer.

The other villager wandered into a cartography table and became a cartographer.

<AssetImage name="d345_FortressCartogropher.png" />

Hunjvo cheered.
“YES! MAPS! BREAD! A REAL VILLAGE!”

The villagers… did not cheer.

But they accepted their fate.

## Snow Villagers

Hunjvo.
