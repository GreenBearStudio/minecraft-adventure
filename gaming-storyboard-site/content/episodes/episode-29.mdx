## Happy Days

The snow villagers were settling in nicely.

The original two plains villagers… not so much.

They smirked at Hunjvo every time he walked by.

“The kidnapper,” they whispered.

“The boat bandit.”

“The man who stole us from the grass.”

But the snow‑born villagers didn’t understand.

To them, “kidnapper” sounded like a heroic title.

“Hunjvo the Kid‑Napper!”  

“One who naps with kids!”

“One who jokes and plays around!”

They adored him.

They followed him.

They told jokes.

They climbed on his head.

They thought he was a legend.

The plains villagers were horrified.

## The Cleric‑Turned‑Farmer

The former cleric — now a farmer — sighed every day.

He missed his purple robe.

He missed brewing potions.

He missed not being kidnapped.

“Maybe Hunjvo will build me a laboratory one day,” he muttered.

Hunjvo, meanwhile, was in the corner eating carrots.

## The Portal Problem

Then the villagers noticed something strange.

Hunjvo was building… something.

<AssetImage name="d353_PortalNotActive.png" />

Something tall.

Something obsidian.

Something glowing.

<AssetImage name="d353_PortalActive.png" />

A portal.

Inside the fortress.

“Hunjvo… what if something comes OUT of it?” the narrator asked.

But Hunjvo didn’t hear.

Or didn’t care.

Or both.

He finished the portal.

<AssetVideo name="d358_FortressPortal.mp4" />

He stepped inside.

He vanished.

## The Nether

The world blurred.

Purple swirls wrapped around him.

Symbols flickered.

Lights flashed.

Then —
FWOMP.

Hunjvo stumbled out into a world of red and black.

The air was hot.

The sky was… not a sky.

The ground glowed.

Giant crimson mushrooms towered overhead.

<AssetVideo name="d353_NetherIntro.mp4" />

“Why is everything red?” Hunjvo whispered.

He turned.

A tall black creature stared at him.

An Enderman.

Then another.

Then three more.

“Don’t look at them,” the narrator whispered.

Hunjvo immediately looked at them.

## The Big Pig

Trying to distract himself, Hunjvo turned toward the crimson forest.

There he saw it.

A giant pig.

Huge.

Snorting.

Covered in tusks.

A Hoglin.

Hunjvo smiled.

“Awwww piggy!”

He reached out to pet it.

“HUNJVO NO!” the narrator screamed.

But Hunjvo was already walking toward the beast.

## Piglins?

From somewhere — maybe the wind, maybe the Nether itself, maybe Noor yelling from a thousand blocks away — Hunjvo heard:

“Those aren’t pigs! They’re Piglins!”

Hunjvo blinked.
“Pig… what?”

He turned around.

A group of Piglins stared at him.

Gold armor.

Crossbows.

Snorting aggressively.

Hunjvo waved.
“Hi!”

The Piglins did not wave back.

## The Adventure Begins

The Nether stretched out before him — crimson forests, warped blue groves, lava oceans, basalt deltas, and dangers everywhere.

Hunjvo had no idea where Noor or Efe were.

He had no idea how he got here.

He had no idea how to get home.

But he did know one thing:

He was about to pet a Hoglin.

And that was probably a mistake.
