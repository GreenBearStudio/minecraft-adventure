Hunjvo found himself in a vast world encircled by towering peaks. The landscape was a tundra forest, dotted with sturdy oak trees whose leaves whispered in the cold breeze. Great flowing waterfalls cascaded down from the high peaks, their waters sparkling in the pale sunlight.

Looking around, Hunjvo felt a mix of awe and uncertainty. What should he do first? After a moment of thought, he decided to climb one of the tallest peaks to get a better view of his surroundings.

## The long way up the Mountain

The climb was challenging, but Hunjvo persevered. When he finally reached the summit, he took in the breathtaking panorama. In the distance, he saw plains and he spotted a small village near a river bed.

Curious and eager for adventure, Hunjvo began his journey down the mountain toward the village, wondering what stories and secrets awaited him there.

## The Village and a Tasty Snack

<AssetImage name="d0_Village.png" alt="Village" />

Curious, Hunjvo approached the village. It was quiet but alive with simple activity. He saw a priest tending to a small shrine and an iron golem standing guard.

As he wandered, Hunjvo met a farmer working the fields. The farmer was kind but focused on his work. When the farmer wasn’t looking, Hunjvo’s hungry stomach got the better of him. He quietly stole some beets from a nearby patch and ate them, feeling the warmth of the food fill him.

With renewed energy, Hunjvo prepared to explore the village further, wondering what adventures awaited him next.
