## The Nether Disaster

Hunjvo was cowering in a corner of the Nether, trembling like a scared rabbit.
A baby Hoglin — tiny, squealing, but somehow still terrifying — was trying to push him off the edge into a lava lake.

“Hunjvo RUN!” the narrator yelled.

Hunjvo bolted.

He sprinted toward the portal — which was, embarrassingly, only a few blocks away.
So yes, Hunjvo had done nothing in the Nether except get bullied by a baby Hoglin.

He leapt into the portal.

Purple swirls.

Strange symbols.

A whooshing sound.

And then—

## Back to the Plains… and a Nightmare

Hunjvo stumbled out into the plains.

“Wait… plains?”

“This isn’t the fortress?”

“Huh?”

But then he saw it.

A zombie horde.

Dozens of them.

Groaning.

Shuffling.

Marching straight toward the Fortress of Fluffy Socks.

“NO!” Hunjvo screamed.

He sprinted up the mountain path.

Zombies were everywhere — climbing walls, breaking doors, chasing villagers.

Hunjvo’s heart dropped.

He leapt into the fray.

He slashed.

He diced.

He spun dramatically (and missed).

He ate a carrot mid‑battle because, well, hunger bars exist.

He jumped off a ledge.

He fell.

He landed on a zombie.

The zombie died.

Hunjvo pretended he meant to do that.

Baby zombies sprinted toward him — tiny, fast, vicious.

<AssetVideo name="d367_FortressUnderAttack3.mp4" />

Hunjvo panicked.

He reached into his pocket for food.

He pulled out…

Rotten zombie flesh.

“HUNJVO!” the narrator yelled.

But Hunjvo ate it anyway.

The baby zombies paused.

They sniffed.

They recoiled.

Hunjvo smelled worse than they did.

## The Turning Tide

Fueled by carrots and questionable dietary choices, Hunjvo fought harder.

He protected the snow villagers.

He rescued the cartographer.

He saved the farmer‑cleric (who immediately complained about everything).

Slowly, the horde thinned.

Zombies fell.

Villagers fled to safety.

The fortress lights glowed again.

Hunjvo stood victorious — sweaty, smelly, exhausted, but victorious.

## Aftermath

The villagers gathered around him.

The snow villagers cheered.

“Hunjvo the Brave!”

“Hunjvo the Protector!”

“Hunjvo the Smelly!”

The plains villagers whispered:
“He kidnapped us… but he saved us too.”
“…Still smells awful though.”

Hunjvo stood proudly, iron sword raised, carrot crumbs on his face.

The Fortress of Fluffy Socks was safe.

For now.
