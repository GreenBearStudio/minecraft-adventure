## A Deep Dark Side Story

During the celebration with Noor and Efe — music playing, torches flickering, Hunjvo doing his questionable butty dance — something strange happened.

A shimmer.

A wobble in the air.

A pop.

And suddenly — Hunjvo vanished.

## A Penguin? A Talking Penguin??

Hunjvo blinked.

He was no longer in the Fortress of Fluffy Socks.

He was… tiny.

Mini‑Hunjvo.

Pocket‑sized.

Fun‑sized.

“What happened?!” Hunjvo screamed inside his own head, because his voice was too small to scream outside of it.

Then he heard a voice.

“Welcome, little player,” said a penguin.

<AssetImage name="Extra_HunjvoMazeRun1Host.png" />

A talking penguin.

Wearing a tiny bow tie.

Standing like a host on a game show.

“I am The Host,” the penguin said proudly.

Hunjvo’s heart raced.

His mind raced faster.

This must be a dream.

It had to be a dream.

A dream with a penguin.

A penguin who talked.

A penguin who—

“So, like I was saying,” the penguin continued, clearly reading Hunjvo’s panicked thoughts, “you were chosen for our Maze Run Challenge!”

## Maze Run… WHAT?!

Hunjvo’s brain exploded into chaos.

Maze run?

Challenge?

Running in circles?

Endless loops?

Getting lost forever?

Noor wasn’t here!

Efe wasn’t here!

Hunjvo couldn’t do this alone!

He began crying visibly — tiny tears, tiny sniffles, tiny trembling arms.

The penguin bowed politely.

“Now now, little player—”

Little.

Player.

Hunjvo suddenly realized he was small.

Very small.

Mini‑Hunjvo.

“Oh no… what—”

“SILENCE!” the penguin snapped, patience finally cracking.

Hunjvo froze.

“You will stand! You will run! And there are prizes for you too!”

Before Hunjvo could protest, the penguin slapped him on the butt — surprisingly hard for a penguin — and Hunjvo went flying straight through a pair of glowing dark doors.

## Music… A Countdown… Other Runners?!

Hunjvo tumbled into a strange arena.

Music blasted.

Lights flashed.

A countdown echoed.

“Now is not the time to think — RUN!” the penguin’s voice boomed from everywhere and nowhere.

Hunjvo looked around.

There were others.

Other tiny players.

Other mini‑adventurers.

Suddenly, he wasn’t scared anymore.

He wasn’t alone.

He wasn’t lost.

He ran.

He followed the crowd.

He dashed into the maze.

A challenge.

A test.

A race.

And then he heard the penguin shout with excitement:

“TREASURE!”

Hunjvo’s eyes sparkled.

“Oh right,” he thought.

And he ran even faster.
