## A bit of Background

Hunjvo is the main character central to the narrative. His journey through the worlds
ties together the themes of exploration, resilience, and mystery.

Originally conceived in the mind of his friend Noor as `Hojgbo`, which the narrator took to
understand as `Hunjbo` or later as `Hunjvo` and which the narrator has gone accustomed to
and hence refered to the main character in this storyboard as `Hun-j-vo`.

## Adventures Await!

Hunjvo likes to build, explore the endless open worlds of Minecraft, and discover mysterious
mysteries of the unknowning known of knowning with unseen forces of friendship, excitement,
and buddy pals like Noor.

Many wonders await... ready to learn, grow, explore!
